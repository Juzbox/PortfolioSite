Bio

Working as a design researcher in emerging markets for corporate and government actors; I've had the chance of engaging with a wide range of topics from access to clean energy, to sexual and reproductive health, to financial inclusion, to innovation at large.

Outside of my more strategic work, I have also enjoyed using design as a means of engaging with broader audiences through public exhibits or cocreation workshops held in schools, museums or makerspaces as a means of challenging the often opaque ways of the design intelligensia.

Design Principles

  1. Build bridges not walls
  2. Design tools not products
  3. Sharpen your accountability
  4. Open-source everything

Research Interests

  1. Access to water and Energy
  2. Urban development and Governance
  3. Data rights and civic engagement
  4. Right to repair + Open source
  5. Design tools not products
